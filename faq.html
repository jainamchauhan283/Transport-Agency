Frequently Asked Questions

It's generally a good idea to book your move at least a few weeks in advance, especially if you are moving during the peak season (May through September). This will give you enough time to plan and prepare for your move. If you have to move urgently, you can connect with moving companies providing last-minute moving services.

There are different ways to get a moving quote:
  • Visit our website and fill out our online form.
  • Give us a call.
Be sure to have an inventory list of your belongings and the distance of your move to get the most accurate quote.

There are several things you can do to prepare for your move:
  • Gather packing supplies such as boxes, tape, and bubble wrap.
  • Declutter your belongings and get rid of items you no longer need.
  • Pack a box of essentials you might need in your new home just after moving in.
  • Arrange for any necessary services to be disconnected or transferred to your new home.
  • Book childcare and pet care services if required.
  • Pack your documents and valuables separately; no mover will carry them on the moving truck.
